About Dr. Yogesh Shelke

Dr. Yogesh Shelke
Assistant Professor
Google Twitter (X) LinkedIn

Yogesh Shelke received his M.Sc. in Organic Chemistry from the University of Pune and completed his Ph.D. in Organic Synthesis at IIT Bombay in 2018 under Prof. Santosh J. Gharpure.

He then worked with Prof. K. C. Nicolaou at Rice University on the total synthesis of anticancer natural products until September 2020. In October 2020, he joined Prof. Martin Burkeâ€™s group at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign focusing on renal-sparing derivatives of amphotericin B, which led to a new antifungal clinical candidate.

In May 2022, he began working at Novo Nordisk Inc. in Boston , focusing on the Medicinal Chemistry of oligonucleotides. Since May 2025, he has been working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemistry at IIT Madras.
